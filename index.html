About GoGreenVue

Built with real communities
in mind

GoGreenVue is a personal project by Vu Pham — a developer who believes great tools should be free, accessible, and fun. Every app here is built with real communities in mind: pickleball players, young learners, and music lovers.

Our Apps

Explore

Music · Sound · Vibe
Soniq
A music companion that syncs lyrics to audio in real time. Paste any lyrics, load your track, and Soniq auto-aligns every line using AI speech recognition — so you always know exactly where you are in the song. Export as a waveform video or download the timed LRC file.
🎵 Open Soniq →
Version 2 · Adaptive
Trivia · Adventure · Ages 5–18
BrainQuest
An adaptive trivia game across Science, Math, Language, Geography, and Nature. Questions automatically match your age group — from Explorer (5–7) up to Master (16–18) — and difficulty scales as you level up. 2,000 hand-crafted questions, no account needed.
⚔️ Play BrainQuest →
Latest Version
Version 3 · Multi-Group
Pickleball RRA
A round-robin tournament organizer built for pickleball communities. Add players, set courts, and let the app generate fair pairings automatically. Supports multiple groups playing at different locations simultaneously, private password-protected sessions, and a live leaderboard.
